Experience

Texas Instruments

Analog Test and Validation Intern• April 2020 - June 2020

Worked on estimation of non linearity of analog front end receiver in the presence of source non linearity. This method of testing can increase the testing speed significantly. Here loopback testing techniques are used such that a digital step attenuator is attached at the output of TX channel and input of feedback channel of the transceiver. Thus transceiver can be tested without any external linear signal source. The error in estimation was as low as -117dBFS.

Projects

Home Automation

Powerline communiation• Agust 2020 - Present

Very cost effective method for home automation i.e. as low as Rs40 per switch. Serial communication between transmitter and receiver causes Logic HIGH and Logic LOW conditions. Logic HIGH occurs when the resonant frequency is present in the frequency spectrum. Bandwidth is close to 1MHz hence enough room for error and thus very less miscommunication and information loss.

Noise Cancelling Headphones

Op Amps• March 2019

Records the unwanted noise from outside and inverts it. Then the inverted signal is phase delayed by approximately 20 degrees. The delay is done in such a manner that it causes destructive interference inside the ear and hence causing the noise to get cancelled.
